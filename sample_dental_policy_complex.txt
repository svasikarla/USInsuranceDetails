COVERED SERVICES
================

CLASS I - PREVENTIVE (100% Coverage)
- Oral examinations (2 per year)
- Routine cleanings (2 per year)  
- Fluoride treatments (age 18 and under)
- Sealants (permanent molars, age 15 and under)
- Space maintainers

CLASS II - BASIC RESTORATIVE (80% Coverage after deductible)
- Fillings (amalgam and composite)
- Simple extractions
- Root canal therapy
- Periodontal scaling and root planing

CLASS III - MAJOR RESTORATIVE (50% Coverage after deductible)
- Crowns and bridges
- Dentures (complete and partial)
- Surgical extractions
- Implants (limited coverage)

ORTHODONTIC COVERAGE (50% Coverage)
- Comprehensive orthodontic treatment
- Limited to dependent children under age 19
- Lifetime maximum: $2,500
- No deductible applies

- Major services: 12-month waiting period
